Why Photography?

When first starting photography, I always wondered what determined good photography, and more precisely, how a photographer could express themselves via simply capturing what already exists. What would set apart my photo of a beautiful place and make that my personal expression? Even though the subject of a photo might be the same for many people, the personal expression comes from capturing “your” moment. Using framing, lighting, angles and countless other tools, you can highlight what matters to you, whether that is the beauty of a scene

In a sense, photography by nature has a much more limiting scope of expression than creating something from scratch, but it's more grounded in reality. This grounding is what draws me towards photography.While most artistic media conveys an experience, photography is unique in its combination of the objective and subjective. Photographers share their experiences by taking the reality of a subject and modifying the way it is perceived through their creativity.
