Welcome!

As the sun sets behind the eiffel tower, I pull out my trusty mirrorless camera, snap on the perfect lens, and lineup the shot, ready to capture this one of a kind moment. But wait, something about this doesn’t look right! Is it too zoomed? Is the angle off? Something else entirely? Perhaps all true, but there’s an even bigger problem: hundreds of other tourists all around you taking the exact same photo! There is nothing to set you apart, nothing unique! What would the people of Instagram think of me!? Welcome to an introduction to photography including everything from the basics of cameras to artistry and post production. This website will introduce you to important concepts that will up your “photo game”! Whether you’re taking a photo for the occasional snapchat, instagram post, or the start of something bigger, this site is a great introduction for an aspiring photographer. The site is designed to teach you everything I wish I knew when starting out and hopefully will be helpful and insightful to everyone who reads it. So, please join in learning the basics of photography; I hope you enjoy learning and reading this website and much as I enjoyed creating and putting together the content.
